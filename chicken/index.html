Why did the Chicken Cross the Road???

  • Originality: What is original about your game?
  • I tried to make frogger but with a chicken trying to cross the road instead.
  • Technical Merit: What is interesting about your game technology?
  • I tried to experiment with Phaser Groups to make the roads and to make the cars. Each car spawns with a different random speed for difficulty.
  • Prototype Postmortem: What did you learn from this prototype? What was the easiest or hardest part of making it?
  • The hardest part was figuring out how groups work with making repeating images. It was hard to get it exactly right, even with the example I found talking about it. It was also difficult to get the collision working for the cars (it still doesn't work) because it seems that group collisions use different physics, while the chicken uses arcade physics.
