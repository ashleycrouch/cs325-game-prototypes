  • Gameplay: The player can move any of the pieces on the board in a diagonal direction, only moving onto black squares. they can only move when they are able to jump over a piece with equal or lesser point value to an empty space. When the player jumps over a piece, the point value times the combo multiplier of the piece is added to the jumping piece. After each turn, a new piece is added randomly to the board. If the player is able to jump over another piece after moving, they may jump the piece and add 1 to the combo multiplier. The combo multiplier is reset to 1 each turn. If the player is unable to move any of the pieces at the start of their turn, then the game is over. The goal is to get the highest point value piece they can. The playing capabilities aren't 100% in yet, as Phaser is confusing. I was able to set up the system for the checkerboard and pieces, but not for jumping and player input.
  • Strategies: There are a few strategies players can try. One is to stack everything into one checker, but the best method seems to be spreading it out and not jumping at every chance you have to do double or triple points. New checkers only spawn one at a time, so going after every point value is sometimes not the best. Try to strategize a bit.
