  • Mandated Variety: I used stargazer and caught! for the mandated variety.
  • Prototype Goal: This prototype is evaluating the classic catching-falling-things mechanic.
  • Player Experience Goals: I just want them to have fun with the whimsicalness of catching stars.
  • Gameplay: The basket moves using the left and right arrow keys. The player must move the basket so that they can catch as many stars as they can before too many of them touch the bottom of the screen. The player has 5 lives, and they lose a life when a star hits the bottom.
  • Strategies: Moving the basket so that it will get to the star in time to catch it.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: In this world, stars are falling from the sky. In order to study the phenomenon, a small child has taken it upon themselves to make a special basket for holding stars and trying to catch as many as they can so that they can study them.
  • Target Audience: The target audience is more towards everyone, it's just supposed to be a game to play real quick while you're bored.
  • Play Time: A few minutes
